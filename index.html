About the Charity Fun Run

Join us for a 5K Charity Fun Run dedicated to bringing the community together for a great cause. Whether you’re a seasoned runner or just out for some fun, this event is open to all ages and fitness levels. Every step you take helps raise funds to support local schools and programs, making a real difference in the lives of families in our community.
